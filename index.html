Experienced Hair Professionals

In 2021, veteran designers Sarah and Chloe opened a new joint venture: S&C Hair Salon.
Our purpose is simple - to provide the highest quality hair cuts paired with stellar service.

Central Location

Located in the heart Etobicoke - minutes away from subway transit and Highway 427. Parking is available in front of the store, with the nearest bus stop at Dundas Street West at Royalavon Crescent.

Variety of Services

S and C provides a wide variety of high quality hair styles and treatment options that suit your budget
